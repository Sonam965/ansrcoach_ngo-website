Our Mission

Worthy Cause Foundation is driven by the vision of a world without suffering. Our mission is to nourish lives, empower communities, respond to emergencies and build a strong movement towards spreading crisis.

WHAT WE DO

We provide life-changing resources such as clean water, medical aid, food, equal educational opportunities to peoples in need. Our view of poverty is holistic and complex. We nourish lives, empower communities, respond to emergencies and build a strong movement towards spreading crisis.
